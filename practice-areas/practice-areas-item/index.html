                  Asbestos and Dust Diseases
                  Your rights and entitlements to compensation for injury and illness as a result of negligent exposure to asbestos and other dusts or chemicals.

                  Medical Negligence
                  Your entitlements to compensation as a result of medical malpractice or negligence.

                  Moto Vehicle Accidents
                  Your entitlements to compensation if involved in a road accident with a car, bike, truck, scooter, any other registered vehicle or as a pedestrian.

                  Public Liability
                  Commonly referred to as "slips and falls", public liability is a civil action for compensation as a result of injury caused by someone else or by a company or, for example, hotel or retail store.

                  Superannuation Insurance Claims
                  Many Australians are not aware that many Superannuation funds hold insurance policies for total and permanent disablement, death benefits and for some, income protection. Learn more here.

                  Work Place Accidents
                  An overview of workers compensation entitlements for workers injured under the State based "Return to Work" scheme and the Federal based "Comcare" scheme.
